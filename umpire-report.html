Location Tendency Definitions
Expansion Direction — horizontal average of stolen strikes (StrikeCalled outside zone). Away to RHH = he expands toward the outside corner; Inside to RHH = he expands toward the inner half; Both Sides = no clear horizontal bias.
Expansion Height — vertical average of stolen strikes relative to the zone. Low = he steals strikes below the zone; High = above; Middle = no clear height preference.
Squeeze Spot — where his lost strikes (BallCalled inside zone) cluster. Tells you which part of the zone he consistently fails to call a strike in. Rare = fewer than 3 lost strikes tracked.
Miss Signature — one-phrase read combining expansion direction and height. Tells your pitcher and catcher exactly where this ump is most likely to reward a well-located pitch outside the zone.
